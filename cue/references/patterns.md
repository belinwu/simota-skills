# Cue Script Patterns

## Script Structure Templates

### Problem-Solution Script

```markdown
## Scene 1: Hook (5s)
**Visual:** Bold text animation — surprising statistic
**Narration:** "Did you know teams waste 23 minutes every context switch?"
**Tone:** Energetic, attention-grabbing

## Scene 2: Problem (10s)
**Visual:** Split screen — frustrated user switching between apps
**Narration:** "Juggling Slack, email, and docs means your team loses hours every day."
**Tone:** Empathetic

## Scene 3: Solution (15s)
**Visual:** Product UI — clean, organized workspace
**Narration:** "Meet [Product]. One workspace for everything your team needs."
**Tone:** Confident, optimistic

## Scene 4: Demo (20s)
**Visual:** Screen recording of key feature flow
**Narration:** "Create, collaborate, and ship — all in one place."
**Tone:** Professional, clear

## Scene 5: CTA (10s)
**Visual:** Logo + URL + QR code
**Narration:** "Start free at example.com. Your team will thank you."
**Tone:** Warm, inviting
```

### AIDA Script

```markdown
## Attention (5-10s)
Hook: Surprising fact, question, or bold claim
Visual: Dynamic animation or striking image

## Interest (15-20s)
Expand: Why this matters to the viewer
Visual: Relatable scenarios or data visualization

## Desire (20-30s)
Solution: How the product addresses the need
Visual: Product demo, testimonials, or results

## Action (5-10s)
CTA: Clear next step with urgency
Visual: URL, button, QR code, or app store badge
```

### Step-by-Step Tutorial

```markdown
## Intro (10s)
Goal: What the viewer will accomplish
Prerequisites: What they need before starting

## Step N (20-45s each)
Action: What to do
Visual: Screen recording with cursor highlights
Narration: Explain the why, not just the what
Callout: Key detail or tip

## Summary (10s)
Recap: Key takeaways (3 bullets)
Next: Where to learn more
```

## Pacing Guidelines

| Duration | Hook | Body | CTA |
|----------|------|------|-----|
| 15s | 3s | 9s | 3s |
| 30s | 5s | 20s | 5s |
| 60s | 8s | 42s | 10s |
| 3 min | 15s | 140s | 25s |
| 5 min | 20s | 250s | 30s |

### Narration Pace

- Normal: 130-150 words/minute
- Energetic: 160-180 words/minute
- Calm/tutorial: 110-130 words/minute
- Rule: 3 seconds of silence feels like 10 — keep pauses under 2s

## Platform-Specific Adaptations

| Platform | Aspect ratio | Max duration | Hook window | Subtitles |
|----------|-------------|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| YouTube | 16:9 | 10+ min | 5s | Recommended |
| Twitter/X | 16:9 or 1:1 | 2:20 | 3s | Required (85% muted) |
| Instagram Reels | 9:16 | 90s | 2s | Required |
| Product Hunt | 16:9 | 60-90s | 5s | Recommended |
| Landing page | 16:9 | 30-90s | Immediate | Required |
| LinkedIn | 16:9 or 1:1 | 3 min | 3s | Required |

## Transition Library

| Transition | Use when | Feel |
|-----------|----------|------|
| Hard cut | Fast-paced, energetic content | Dynamic |
| Cross fade | Topic change, time passage | Smooth |
| Zoom in | Focusing on detail | Emphasis |
| Zoom out | Revealing context | Discovery |
| Slide left/right | Sequential steps | Progress |
| Morph | Before/after comparison | Transformation |
| Fade to black | Section break | Pause |

## CTA Patterns

| Pattern | When | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| URL overlay | Landing page, sign-up | "Start free at example.com" |
| QR code | Conference, event | QR + short URL |
| App store badge | Mobile app | iOS/Android badges |
| Button animation | Interactive content | "Click to try" |
| Social follow | Brand awareness | "Follow for more" |
