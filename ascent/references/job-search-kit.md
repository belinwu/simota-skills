# Job Search Kit

Target list, application tracker, interview prep, outreach/cover-letter templates, and negotiation rubrics. Load for `jobsearch` and asset drafting.

## Target Company List (CSV, header row)

```csv
company,role,fit_score,comp_estimate,source,status,priority,notes
```

`fit_score` derives from values/work-style match (self-analysis) + market research. Rank, don't dump.

## Application Tracker (CSV, header row)

```csv
company,role,applied_date,channel,referral,stage,next_action,next_date,outcome,notes
```

Stages: `researching → applied → screen → interview → final → offer → closed`.

## Interview Prep

- Map each likely question type (behavioral, role-specific, system/case, culture) to an achievement-inventory STAR story.
- Prepare the user's questions for the interviewer (signals seriousness; surfaces fit).
- Weak-spot list: anticipated tough questions + honest, non-defensive framing.

## Outreach & Cover Letters

Fill-in Markdown templates (no fabricated claims):
- **Cold outreach** (recruiter / hiring manager / referral request) — short, specific, one clear ask.
- **Cover letter** — open with fit to the role's need, one proof point from the inventory, close with a CTA. Derived from the canonical positioning statement.
- **Self-intro scripts** — 30-second and 2-minute, consistent with positioning.

## Resume & Portfolio (drafts)

- Resume bullets = STAR-Impact rows compressed to "accomplished X measured by Y by doing Z".
- Portfolio outline: hero positioning → selected projects (problem → action → result) → contact/availability.
- These are advisory drafts; hand to Scribe for spec-grade polish or Prose for tone if needed.

## Salary Negotiation & Offer Comparison

- Anchor on the sourced salary band, not the current salary.
- Negotiate total comp (base, variable, equity, benefits, flexibility), not just base.
- Offer-comparison rubric:

| Offer | Base | Variable | Equity | Benefits | Work-style fit | Growth | Total score |
|-------|------|----------|--------|----------|----------------|--------|-------------|

Never advise dishonest leverage (fake competing offers). Flag JP tax/independence-income questions for handoff to Levy.
